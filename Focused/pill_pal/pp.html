The Details.

Pill Pal is a two part system, a smart ring and an app, that helps users improve and maintain good medicine adherence. The ring discreetly rests on the finger of the user and reminds them by discreetly glowing and vibrating to take their medicine. The ring also utilizes RFID technology between the ring and the userâ€™s pill bottles to confirm taking a dosage. The app connects to the ring to allow the user to adjust haptic levels as well as manage what pills the ring should remind them to take. The user can also take a look at their medicine adherence over time to see their progress.

Heart disease is the leading cause of the death in the US and not having good medicine adherence will lead to consequences. This system is to help manage the average 6.8 medications users with heart disease would have to take. Many of the decisions that led to this final concept was driven by interviews from both users who have been prescribed medications and caretakers. This was a group project in which I contributed much of the product design and brand development. More about our design process can be read in our process book above.
