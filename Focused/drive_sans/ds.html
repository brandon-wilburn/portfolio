The Details.

Drive Sans is a typeface built entirely from paths and stroke animations. All of the letters were created in Illustrator using a 3x4 grid and then transported to HTML. Using AnimeJS, all of the paths have their own independent animation. On hover, the letters increase in width to give the appearance of being brighter and more saturated.
